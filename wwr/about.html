Harris River Rafting

Our Journey

Founded in 2024 by Joe Harris, Harris River Rafting began as a weekend adventure among friends in the Rocky Mountains. What started with a single raft and a love for whitewater quickly grew into a full-fledged rafting company. Today, we're dedicated to offering thrilling and memorable river experiences, blending excitement with a deep appreciation for nature. Join us and ride the rapids with a team passionate about adventure!
