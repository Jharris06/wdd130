History

Founded in 2024, Harris River Rafting started as a small adventure club in the heart of the Rocky Mountains, with just a handful of enthusiastic rafters and a single boat. What began as a weekend hobby quickly evolved into a thriving business, fueled by a passion for whitewater adventure and a commitment to providing unparalleled experiences on the river.
